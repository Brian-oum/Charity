About Us

At the heart of the Christine Otieno Foundation is a deep passion for music and ministry — expressed through sharing God’s love in both song and service. Founded by celebrated gospel artist Christine Otieno, the foundation believes that true ministry goes beyond the stage. It is about touching lives, meeting needs, and bringing hope to communities across Kenya.

Sharing God’s Love Through Action

Christine Otieno has led impactful mission outreaches across Kenya, uplifting communities through initiatives like building a church in Lodiba, providing food to families, and distributing sanitary pads to young girls to support their education. In Nairobi, her foundation runs weekly feeding programs for street families and organizes gospel crusades and Bible distribution missions to spread faith and encouragement.

Every year, the foundation hosts a special outreach in Siaya County for widows, offering essential items, financial support, and spiritual care. Christine also uses music as a tool for transformation, earning recognition in the gospel industry, but her greatest reward is seeing lives restored, families strengthened, and hope renewed through Christ.

HOW OUR PROGRAMS WORK

The Christine Otieno Foundation designs programs that uplift communities through faith, service, and empowerment. Each initiative is carefully planned to meet real needs, inspire hope, and foster lasting transformation.

Identify Community Needs

  • Assess unique challenges and needs of each community.
  • Prioritize initiatives based on urgency and impact.

Plan & Organize Outreach

  • Arrange resources, volunteers, and logistics efficiently.
  • Set timelines and milestones for smooth execution.

Community Engagement

  • Involve community members actively in the programs.
  • Encourage participation and foster collaboration.

Deliver Support & Services

  • Provide direct aid such as food, sanitary items, and spiritual care.
  • Ensure programs are inclusive and reach the most vulnerable.

Monitor & Evaluate

  • Track program progress and impact regularly.
  • Adjust activities based on feedback to maximize effectiveness.

Celebrate Impact

  • Recognize transformed lives and strengthened families.
  • Share success stories to inspire hope and community participation.

Christine Otieno Foundation is dedicated to empowering vulnerable communities through education, healthcare, and social support programs across Kenya.
