Health Programs

The Christine Otieno Foundation improves the health of vulnerable communities across Kenya through targeted medical outreach, education, and maternal-child support programs.

Medical Outreach

We provide free check-ups, vaccinations, and treatments to underserved communities, ensuring families receive essential healthcare and early disease prevention.

Health Education

We conduct workshops on hygiene, nutrition, disease prevention, and reproductive health, empowering communities to adopt healthier lifestyles.

Maternal & Child Health

Supporting pregnant women and children with antenatal care, nutrition support, and essential medical supplies to promote safe pregnancies and healthy growth.

Women & Girls Empowerment

The Christine Otieno Foundation is a Christ-centered organization dedicated to uplifting women and girls across Kenya. Inspired by gospel artist Christine Otieno, the foundation promotes education, dignity, and self-reliance through targeted empowerment initiatives and practical support programs.

Supporting Girls’ Education & Dignity

Christine Otieno and her team actively distribute sanitary pads to young girls in schools and rural communities, ensuring they can attend classes with confidence and dignity. These programs aim to reduce school absenteeism and empower girls to continue their education without interruption.

In addition to menstrual hygiene support, the foundation provides mentorship, workshops, and life skills training for adolescent girls . By equipping them with knowledge and confidence, the foundation nurtures the next generation of leaders and change-makers.

Empowering Women in Communities

The foundation supports women through skills development, vocational training , and income-generating projects. By providing these resources, women gain financial independence, confidence, and the ability to support their families and communities.

Through annual outreach events and ongoing programs, the Christine Otieno Foundation creates opportunities for women and girls to thrive. From education support to practical life skills and spiritual encouragement, the foundation’s mission is to foster self-reliance, hope, and empowerment at every level.

Our Frequent Programs

Feeding Street Families

Weekly meals and care packages for street families in Nairobi, offering nourishment, encouragement, and hope to those in need.

Sanitary Pads for Girls

Distributing pads to school girls in rural and urban areas, empowering them to attend school confidently and maintain dignity.

Gospel Crusades

Organizing weekly gospel crusades and Bible distributions to spread God’s word and spiritual encouragement across communities.

Music Outreach

Using gospel music as a tool for transformation, the foundation hosts music sessions and performances that inspire faith, hope, and positive change.

Education Empowerment

The Christine Otieno Foundation is a Christ-centered organization committed to supporting children and young adults from underprivileged backgrounds. Inspired by gospel artist Christine Otieno, the foundation promotes education, dignity, and personal development through targeted programs and practical support.

Supporting Students’ Learning & Growth

Christine Otieno Foundation actively provides scholarships, school supplies, and learning materials to students from low-income families. By easing financial barriers, the foundation enables children to attend school regularly, pursue their studies diligently, and achieve academic success.

Beyond material support, the foundation runs mentorship programs, study groups, and workshops that equip students with essential life skills, leadership training, and confidence. These programs nurture young minds to become responsible, empowered individuals ready to contribute positively to their communities.

Empowering Communities Through Education

The Christine Otieno Foundation is dedicated to improving access to education for children and youth in underserved communities. By providing scholarships, school supplies, and learning materials, the foundation ensures that every child has the opportunity to attend school, learn effectively, and reach their full potential.

In addition to material support, the foundation runs mentorship programs, tutoring sessions, and educational workshops. These initiatives equip students with critical thinking skills, confidence, and life skills, fostering a culture of learning and personal growth throughout the community.

Christine Otieno Foundation is dedicated to empowering vulnerable communities through education, healthcare, and social support programs across Kenya.
