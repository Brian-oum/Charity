Sharing God’s Love Through Action

The Christine Otieno Foundation is built on a strong commitment to compassion, service, and community upliftment. Through various initiatives—such as feeding programs for street families, community support visits, and faith-centered outreach—we strive to reflect God’s love in action. Our programs are designed to restore dignity, bring hope, and meet practical needs within the communities we serve.

Guided by integrity, excellence, and a belief in the power of collective impact, the foundation continues to invest in long-term transformation. We support communities through educational engagement, spiritual empowerment, and acts of kindness that strengthen families. Every mission, every outreach, and every act of service is rooted in our desire to build a society where love, unity, and opportunity flourish through Christ.

OUR CORE VALUES

The Christine Otieno Foundation is built upon values that guide every outreach, program, and act of service. These core principles ensure that our mission remains impactful, compassionate, and centered on uplifting communities.

Compassion

  • We serve with empathy, love, and genuine care for every individual.
  • We go beyond material support by offering emotional and spiritual encouragement.

Community

  • We believe growth happens when communities stand together and support one another.
  • Every program strengthens unity, belonging, and collective responsibility.

Integrity

  • We uphold honesty and transparency in all our actions and decisions.
  • Resources are used responsibly to ensure trust and accountability.

Service

  • We are committed to serving vulnerable families with dignity and respect.
  • Our actions reflect our calling to impact lives through kindness and generosity.

Education

  • We empower individuals through knowledge, learning, and mentorship.
  • We believe education is the foundation for transforming communities.

Excellence

  • We strive for high standards in everything we do, big or small.
  • Every initiative is delivered with passion, professionalism, and purpose.

Christine Otieno Foundation is dedicated to empowering vulnerable communities through education, healthcare, and social support programs across Kenya.
