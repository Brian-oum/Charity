About Us

The Christine Otieno Foundation is a Christ-centered, compassion-driven organization dedicated to transforming lives across Kenya. Inspired by the ministry and music of gospel artist Christine Otieno, the foundation shares God’s love through practical support, community outreach, and life-changing mission work. We believe that ministry extends beyond the stage—it is expressed through service, kindness, and hope.

We Are Rooted in Our Communities

Over the years, Christine Otieno and her team have carried out impactful missions across Kenya—touching lives in both rural and urban communities. In Turkana County, the foundation helped build a church in Lodiba, provided food to families facing hunger, and offered sanitary pads to young girls so they can pursue their education with dignity and confidence.

In Nairobi, the foundation runs a weekly feeding program supporting street families with meals, encouragement, and spiritual care. Through gospel crusades, music ministry, and Bible distribution drives, the foundation continues to spread the message of love, faith, and salvation across different regions of the country.

Give a Future Full of Hope

Every year, the foundation hosts a special outreach event in Siaya County, where widows receive essential supplies, financial assistance, and spiritual nourishment. This annual gathering has restored dignity, strength, and joy to countless women who often face deep social and economic challenges.

Christine Otieno’s dedication to using music as a tool for transformation has earned her numerous awards and recognitions in the gospel industry. However, for the foundation, the greatest reward is seeing lives restored, hearts healed, and hope reborn. Our mission is simple—to spread the love of Christ through action and community service, one act of kindness at a time.
